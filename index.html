Case Study 1

How a trusted Optical Store used SAP Hybris to grow their Online Sales

Regarded as one of the most trusted opticians, the Company is renowned for the exceptional quality standards that it sets in precision Eye-Care globally. They call themselves “Visionary” Pioneers in the Optical Industry and have been in existence for over 100 years having served generations for all the eye-wear needs with a steadfast commitment towards Long-term growth and Quality & Care in Service. With over 5000+ Stores globally, their name has become synonymous with all Eye-care and Optical requirements needs. Today, the Company is leading provider of International Brands in the space of Eye-wear (Cosmetic and Spectacles), Fashionable Sunglasses, Best-in-Class Contact Lenses and Computerized eye-testing.

Challenge: Traditionally being a Retail-store oriented business, the Company wanted to cater to the online market owing to the change in Consumer’s Buying Behavior. Along with a Technology integration into their existing eyewear business, the Company wanted to bring in various services along with it to cater to the ever changing Consumer demands like Customized Eyewear, Virtual ‘Wear and Try’ for their Customers, Online Purchase of Spectacles & Doorstep Eye-check Up for their Customers by their growing pool of Opticians.

Being an existent user of SAP within their organization and also having included a legacy CRM solution, the Company was looking to upgrade to a more robust & scalable Omni-channel Commerce Solution to serve its new and expanding Customer base and its new sophisticated business requirements. After reviewing various solutions in the market, the Company decided to go ahead with SAP Hybris’ Commerce solution which would not only be an ideal fit to cater to its new business direction, but would also make its integration with the current SAP implementation easier thus ensuring that all its business processes like Supply Chain, Logistics, Sales, Finance etc., are perfectly streamlined.

Why SAP Hybris?

  • Ability to Scale based on the changing Business needs and Consumer Behavior
  • Unified and integrated system for Content Management, Online Ordering and Supply Chain integration
  • Quick time to Value and Integration
  • An Intuitive and Superior Mobile Friendly experience for the Customers
  • Supports multiple languages helping cater to customers globally
  • Easier Price Quote management owing to Customized and different Customer requirements
  • Supports new product launches and promotions across various channels

Results:

  • Through a structured and well managed Deployment procedure, the Go-live took less than 6 months. This was deployed and monitored by experienced SAP Hybris experts.
  • Regularly updated Online Inventory
  • A more streamlined Complaints, Feedbacks, Customer Support and Repair/Service mechanism
  • Increased Customer Orders due to an Online Presence and more so, due a Mobile-focused approach
  • Increased Efficiency, more streamlined and standardized Business Processes
  • Significant increase in presence and awareness across various Social Media channels

How one of the largest manufacturers of water heaters streamlined their Customer Support ops using SAP Hybris

The Company is one of the largest manufacturer & supplier of Water Heaters. With over 6 decades of innovative experience in water heating technology, the Company manufactures gas and electric storage, heat pump and solar water heaters for domestic, commercial and large scale project applications with a global footprint for exports. Manufacturing and Sales being there core areas of Business, the Company also offers thoroughly professional grade Repair and Service, thus furthering Customer Retention and Satisfaction.

Challenge: The Company’s Service division receives a large number of requests each day towards Service, Repairs, Installation Requests, and Customer Complaints. They were not only looking for a solution that could streamline this process, but also handle peak traffic during high volume seasons, especially the winters and pre-winters time. Their existing systems were incapable of handling large volume requests and they were seeing a fall in the customer retention. They were seeing a reduced promptness in responding to Customer inquiries and were unable to fully understand the Outcomes of the Customer queries for future process improvements. They wanted a quick solution to this issue and where intent in making improvements in the way they handle the calls, respond and offer advice.

Using the consult of our highly experienced SAP experts and being an existing SAP user, the company decided that SAP Hybris Cloud for Customer (C4C) was the best fit for their needs to manage Customer Sales, Customer Service and Marketing Activities.

Results:

  • With a Mobile-first approach, SAP Cloud for Customer meets the requirements of today's sales and service professionals who need information about customers any time and in any location. 
  • Over a 100 people of their staff and external partners now use C4C from SAP, with atleast 30 staff using it on a daily basis to work on Customer issues and Satisfaction even during peak business season.
  • Due to an enhanced User Experience, the Staff is now more focused on higher productivity out of the tool rather than spending endless hours in entering information
  • Through the tool, the employees are now easily and quickly able to resolve customer problems and are able to deliver a highly personalized flavor of customer engagement resulting in higher customer satisfaction and revenues
  • The Call center staff are now handling calls faster and adding more value, with an average 15-20% savings in call times
  • Comprehensive Outcome tracking: Deeper understanding of the type of work being assigned to the service professionals, understanding the quality of service delivery, easier re-allotment of tasks to another an agent in case the previous agent is busy and easier tracking of the customer’s satisfaction levels for the service
Case Study 4

A Large Indian Telecom company transforms their Customer Experience through SAP Hybris Billing Solutions

A large Telecom company based out of India, and operating in over 15 locations globally, was looking to upgrade their existing SAP Systems to meet the requirements of their rapidly increasing User subscriptions. Their services included fixed telephony, Pre-paid cards, Post-paid plans all having varying levels of Data needs depending on the consumer’s needs and fixed line, fiber and broadband services.

Challenge: The company was looking for a Unified Billing solution that could encompass the highly variegated billing scenarios of its customers. Their existing CRM systems were unable to comprehend such diverse billing scenarios and due to the limitations of flexibility in its billing functionalities, it restricted their Business teams in having the first-mover advantage through innovative Products and Service plans to its customers in a highly competitive marketplace. It was imperative for them to offer and have an in-depth market understanding of the outcomes of their various postpaid, prepaid and other offerings.

Furthermore, they were looking to streamline the information flow between their various business units and further bottom-lines and have a competitive edge in the market. They also wanted to ensure that the Customer Service and Management units run more smoothly to boost their Customer retention.

Why SAP Hybris?

The obvious solution for the company was the SAP Hybris Billing Solution which armed them with a unified and a completely automated way of managing their billing processes. The Solution could

  • Encompass multiple billing streams and process them into a single invoice
  • Capture and include any revenue sharing agreements
  • Could handle One-time or Recurring billing for their service offerings
  • Include Various offers and discounts

Results:

As a result of the SAP Hybris Billing Implementation, the Company was able to:

  • Enable real-time charging and rating of services
  • Reduce time to market for their new offerings
  • Facilitate an end-to-end process flow for all the business functions resulting in increased internal collaboration
  • Gain a holistic view of all customer accounts and their subscribed services
  • Improve Customer Experience, Service Satisfaction and User Retention
